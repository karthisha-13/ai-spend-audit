# Economics

## Problem
Users spend money on multiple AI tools without tracking usage.

## Target Users
- Students
- Freelancers
- Small teams
- Startups

## Solution
AI Spend Audit helps users analyze spending and identify cost-saving opportunities.

## Pricing Strategy
- Free: Basic audit
- Pro: Advanced insights + recommendations
- Team: Multi-user tracking

## Revenue Model
Subscription-based SaaS model.

## Value Proposition
- Save money
- Improve efficiency
- Better decision making
