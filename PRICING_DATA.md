# Pricing Data

## Overview

AI Spend Audit follows a SaaS-based pricing model designed to support different user segments, from individual users to teams.

---

## Plans

### 1. Free Plan

- Basic spend audit functionality  
- Limited usage per day  
- No history tracking  

Best suited for:
- Students  
- First-time users exploring the product  

---

### 2. Pro Plan (Planned)

- Advanced spending insights  
- Detailed savings recommendations  
- Audit history tracking  
- Improved and personalized suggestions  

Best suited for:
- Freelancers  
- Individual professionals  

---

### 3. Team Plan (Planned)

- Multi-user access  
- Shared dashboards  
- Team-level spending insights  
- Usage tracking across members  

Best suited for:
- Startups  
- Small teams  

---

## Pricing Strategy

- Begin with a free plan to reduce entry barriers and attract users  
- Introduce Pro features that provide deeper value and insights  
- Expand to team-based plans for scalable revenue growth  

---

## Revenue Logic

- Free users convert to paid plans after experiencing value  
- Pro users generate consistent individual revenue  
- Team plans contribute higher revenue through multiple users  

---

## Why This Model Works

- Low barrier to entry encourages adoption  
- Clear value-driven upgrade path  
- Scalable across individuals and teams  
- Aligns with common SaaS growth strategies  

---

## Future Improvements

- Introduce usage-based pricing models  
- Add custom enterprise plans  
- Integrate secure billing and subscription management  
- Offer trial periods for premium features
