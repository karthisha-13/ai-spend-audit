# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target Audience

- Students using AI tools for learning and projects  
- Freelancers relying on AI for productivity  
- Small teams and early-stage startups  

---

## Problem Awareness

Many users subscribe to multiple AI tools but do not actively track their total monthly spending.  
This leads to unnecessary costs and inefficient usage.

---

## Launch Strategy

- Share the product on LinkedIn targeting student and developer communities  
- Post in relevant online communities such as Reddit, Discord, and WhatsApp groups  
- Create a short demo video showcasing how users can identify and reduce unnecessary spending  

---

## User Acquisition

- Offer the tool for free to attract initial users  
- Keep the interface simple and easy to use  
- Encourage sharing through word-of-mouth  

---

## Growth Strategy

- Continuously improve insights and recommendations  
- Introduce a Pro version with advanced analytics  
- Expand features for team-based usage and collaboration  

---

## Why This Strategy Works

- AI tool adoption is rapidly increasing  
- Cost optimization is a real and relatable problem  
- Simple and useful tools have high chances of organic growth  

---

## Future Expansion

- Integrate real pricing data from AI platforms  
- Add personalized recommendations  
- Build a subscription tracking system  
- Enable usage analytics and reporting
